Bit Bang (CS1951a-final-project)

Welcome to our cs1951a project website. Our project group is called Bit Bang.

In our project, we tried predicting the price fluctuation of the currency bitcoin through different approaches.

Some of the approaches were a trading indicator approach, a macroeconomic approach and a mining rate trend approach.

You can follow our progress by reading the blog posts in the side bar and see our final report as well. Enjoy!
